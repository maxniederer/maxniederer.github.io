About

Max's background is in computer science but he is an artist at heart; whether through illustration, writing, or game development, he is a maker who loves to create projects that highlight the underrepresented.

His first love is video games, shortly seconded by watching movies, reading, playing tabletop games, and watching anime. His favorite stories are Yakuza 0, Berserk, If We Were Villains, and Perfect Blue.

He creates short indie games under the name Specium. You can find his games on his itch.io profile.

He is based near Philadelphia (willing to relocate) and looking for employment in games and software. If interested in working together, you may view his resume or email him at niederermaxwell@gmail.com. Do not contact him about blockchain or AI-generated projects.
