LanguageQuest

The Gwich'in people are a First Nations people of Canada and an Alaskan native tribe. Through recent territorial disputes and English-centric modern society, the number of speakers of the Gwich'in language has dindled down to only 300 current speakers today.

LanguageQuest was created to preserve the culture and language of the Gwich'in people. While the player walks around a Gwich'in camp and completes normal everyday tribe tasks, new Gwich'in words are added to the dictionary. The player can learn words and phrases through talking to NPCs and playing language mini-games.

The demo encompasses one chapter of the game and comes complete with the Gwich'in camp environment, numerous NPCs to talk to, a fishing minigame, a caribou hunting minigame, and a quiz.

This was created in 4 months with a team of 3 other developers.
